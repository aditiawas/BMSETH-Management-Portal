About The Hostel

Best thing that happened these four years? Maybe hostel. It was a great experience, I loved it.

- Ex-Hostelite

The BMS Education Trust (BMSET) is a prestigious institution which founded and manages the top class colleges viz., BMS College of Engineering (BMSCE) , BMS Institute of Technology & Management, BMS College of Law, BMS College for women and others institutions. BMSET Hostels are part of BMSET and BMSET Hostels in the campus of BMS College of Engineering (BMSCE) provide accommodation to the students wishing to reside in the hostels. BMSET Hostels gives a distinct advantage of befriending with students from across the globe and expose them different religions, languages and customs of countries. Additionally, living as a community in the hostel will lead to all-round development of personality of a hostelite and will definitely go a long way in fostering world-class citizens. Hostels are independent units in respects to its internal administration under the overall supervision of council of wardens and the hostel management. The hostels are located in the southern side and adjacent to the college campus and have a separate hostel office and mess within the hostel premises.
